My top 3 Movies of all time.

Inception

I love christopher Nolan and thrillers. The concept of shared dreams and dreams within other dreams has always been crazy to me. He's such a goated director.

The Shawshank Redemption

This movie is also awesome. Such a feel good classic. The innocent man framed for his wife's murder and sent to prison. What will happen..

Dead Poet's Society

Robin Williams portrayal of an english teacher at a preppy New England school teaches the kids to be themselves and embrace doing what they love. A very heartfelt performance and movie alike.
